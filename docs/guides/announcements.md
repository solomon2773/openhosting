# Announcements

OpenHosting includes a lightweight news/blog module for posting product updates,
maintenance notices and company news to your storefront.

## Managing posts

Under **Admin → Announcements** you create and edit posts with:

- **Title**
- **Excerpt** — a short summary shown in the list
- **Body** — plain text or basic HTML
- **Published** — unpublished posts are drafts and stay hidden

## On the storefront

Published posts appear at `/blog`, linked as **News** in the storefront
navigation. Each post has its own page at `/blog/<slug>`.

## Use cases

- Scheduled-maintenance and incident notices
- New product or feature announcements
- Policy or pricing changes
- General company updates

Posts are ordered newest-first by publish date. Drafts let you prepare an
announcement ahead of time and publish it when ready.
