# Contacts & sub-accounts

Customers can add additional **contacts** to their account — team members,
billing departments, technical staff — each with scoped access and an optional
login.

## Managing contacts

Customers manage their own contacts under **Account → Contacts & sub-accounts**.
Each contact has:

- **Name and email**
- **Password** (optional) — set one to let the contact log in as a sub-account;
  leave it blank for a notification-only contact
- **Permissions** — any of: view invoices, manage tickets, view services,
  manage account

## Use cases

- A **billing contact** who only sees invoices
- A **technical contact** who manages support tickets and services
- A notification-only contact who receives account emails without a login

## Notes

- A contact's email must be unique — it can't match an existing full account or
  another contact.
- Removing a contact revokes their access immediately.
